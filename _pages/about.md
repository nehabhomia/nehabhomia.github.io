I am a data and systems professional working at the intersection of healthcare, analytics, reporting, and technical implementation. My work focuses on building reliable data pipelines, validating complex healthcare datasets, automating reporting workflows, and translating technical findings into clear, actionable information for clinical, operational, and administrative stakeholders.

I currently support data infrastructure and reporting work across the University of Michigan and Michigan Medicine, with experience in healthcare quality improvement, vendor data integrations, performance measure reporting, dashboard development, and stakeholder-facing analytics.

My technical work includes Python, pandas, SQL, JSON processing, API integrations, data validation, automated reporting, and documentation for reproducible analytics workflows. I am especially interested in data quality, transparency, lineage, and building systems that are maintainable beyond a single analyst or developer.

I also teach graduate-level healthcare data analytics, where I help students develop practical skills in data cleaning, analysis, interpretation, and communication using real-world healthcare contexts.

## Areas of Focus

- Healthcare data analytics

- Data pipeline design and validation

- Quality improvement reporting

- Python automation and pandas workflows

- Vendor data integration

- Data documentation and reproducibility

- Stakeholder communication
